Locating Thari Lounge

Transit Directions From SSKIA

We are located just 3 minutes away from the main terminal doors of Sir Seretse Khama International Airport, making us the perfect transit hub.

  • 🚌 Lounge Shuttle: Free automated shuttle departing every 20 minutes from the Arrivals bus bay.
  • 🚗 By Road: Exit the airport terminal roundabout, take your immediate first left into Airport Corporate Park.
